About Stevens

The Stevens Institute of Technology is a leading innovation university,  founded in 1870.  There is a great value in Higher Education,  including increased career potential and quality of life.  We work to make high-quality education worthwhile, relevant and accessible.


Public good,  in the context of a top-tier comprehensive university,  includes research,  teaching and service by which we create knowledge foe and with the public.  We engage eith problems that affect the public,  and we enable each generation of citizens to remake our democracy by equipping them with skills of critical reasoning and analysis,  and a broad sense of civic obligation to the state, the nation,and the world.
